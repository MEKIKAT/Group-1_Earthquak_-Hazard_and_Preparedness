Mitigation Plan

Unexpected earthquakes can happen at any time and have the power to significantly damage both people as well as structures. Evaluate your home's and workplace's earthquake risks and take appropriate measures. Among these mitigation measures should be:

  • Storing bulky, fragile, or heavy items on lower shelves.
  • Securing large items like shelves, bookcases, and cabinets to walls and floors.
  • Avoid placing heavy objects near places where people commonly sit or congregate, such as mirrors and portraits.
  • Fixing faulty gas connections and electrical wiring. This could cause a fire.
  • Repairing any severe structural or ceiling fractures. If there are indications of structural issues, seek professional guidance.
